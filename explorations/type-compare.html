How to read this A is what you have — beautiful, and instantly legible as AI. B keeps the premium editorial feel but trades Playfair's cold Didone for a warmer, more crafted serif, and drops the mono eyebrow for a humanist sans — same lane, no longer the default. C leaves the editorial lane for a confident, sans-led product identity that no image model reaches for by reflex. Emphasis is colour and weight only now, no italics — italic-on-every-headline was itself one of the AI tells, so dropping it is a feature, not a compromise.
